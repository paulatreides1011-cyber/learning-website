Faculty of Engineering

Our IT Engineering department prepares students for the rapidly evolving tech industry through hands-on training, cutting-edge research, and industry partnerships. Students gain expertise in software development, network security, and emerging technologies.

Programs Offered:
  • B.S. in Software Engineering
  • B.S. in Cybersecurity
  • B.S. in Data Science & AI
  • M.S. in Computer Science
  • Ph.D. in Information Technology

Institute For International Studies and Public Policy

The Institute for International Studies and Public Policy prepares students to understand global affairs, public governance, and international cooperation. Through innovative education, research, and practical learning experiences, students develop critical thinking, leadership, and analytical skills for careers in diplomacy, public service, policy development, and international organizations.

Programs Offered:
  • B.A. in International Relations
  • B.A. in Public Policy
  • B.A. in Political Science
  • B.A. in Development Studies
  • M.A. in International Studies
Learn More
Institute of Foreign Languages

Our Institute of Foreign Languages offers comprehensive programs in literature, linguistics, and professional communication. Students develop critical thinking, analytical, and communication skills essential for diverse career paths in education, media, publishing, and beyond.

Programs Offered:
  • B.A. in English Literature
  • B.A. in Applied Linguistics
  • B.A. in Creative Writing
  • M.A. in English Studies
  • TESOL Certification
Learn More
Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science provides students with a strong foundation in scientific knowledge, research, and innovation. Through comprehensive programs in mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and environmental science, students develop analytical, problem-solving, and critical-thinking skills essential for careers in research, education, technology, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.

Programs Offered:
  • B.S. in Mathematics
  • B.S. in Physics
  • B.S. in Chemistry
  • M.A. in Biology
  • M.S. in Environmental Science
